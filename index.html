Event Timeline

World Leader Dr. Ambedkar International Award

Organized to mark the 135th birth anniversary of Dr. B. R. Ambedkar, held from May 8 to May 10, 2026, at the School of Oriental and African Studies (SOAS) University of London.

Day 1 • May 8, 2026 Seminar

International Academic Seminar

SOAS University of London

Gathered scholars, jurists, and human rights researchers on a global platform to discuss constitutional justice, equality, and democratic representation, and to translate local civil rights struggles into an international framework.

Day 2 • May 9, 2026 Networking

International Business Seminar

SOAS University of London

Facilitated sessions with corporate executives, venture capitalists, and global entrepreneurs. Aimed at fostering minority business ecosystems, strengthening trade routes, and encouraging economic self-reliance.

Day 3 • May 10, 2026 Ceremony

Ambedkar Gaurav Sohala

SOAS University of London

Celebrated Dr. Ambedkar's civil rights legacy with cultural events, speeches, and the presentation of international awards recognizing global and local advocates.

Chief Guest

Justice C. L. Thul

Former Judicial Commissioner

Justice C. L. Thul, the former Chairman of the Maharashtra Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes Commission and the former Chairman of the Maharashtra State Human Rights Commission, attended as Chief Guest. With a background in management and leadership from Washington D.C., his presence helped connect academic legal concepts with practical human rights enforcement in India.

Alliances & Coalitions

Geopolitical Alliances & Buddhist Networks

The international expansion of the Vishwa Leader platform is built on working partnerships with Buddhist organizations and global representatives, helping turn regional civil rights campaigns into international conversations.

Diplomacy

Tibetan Parliament-in-Exile

Shirish Bhageshwar Ramteke led a delegation of Ambedkarites to visit the Tibetan parliamentary headquarters in Dharamshala on November 3, 2025, meeting with Deputy Speaker Dolma Tsering Teykhang to discuss social justice and religious freedom.

Official Statement
Delegations

State Advocacy Tour

Shirish Ramteke and Sandesh Meshram (Samten Yeshi), president of the Indo-Tibetan Friendship Association, hosted Tibetan MPs in Mumbai in January 2025. The tour included meetings with state political leaders like Ajit Pawar, filmmaker Shyam Benegal, and local social activists.

Siddharth College Lectures

Coordinated academic lectures on Buddhist philosophy and human rights at Siddharth College of Arts, Science, and Commerce, an institution founded by Dr. Ambedkar. This collaboration helped build institutional partnerships across social movements.
