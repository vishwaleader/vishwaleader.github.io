Academic Invitation

Calling for Articles: SOAS University Conference 2026

Held on the eve of the Vishwa Leader Dr. B. R. Ambedkar International Awards 2026 in London, UK. The organizing committee invites scholars, researchers, and activists to submit abstracts and full research papers for the academic conference at SOAS University of London.

Primary Theme

"Reimagining Equality and Justice: Dr. B. R. Ambedkar's Vision in the 21st Century"

Innovative Solutions for Inclusive Societies

Proposing new ideas and practical solutions to foster equality and address modern governance issues.

Social Justice and Human Rights

Exploration of Ambedkar's perspectives on justice and their relevance to contemporary human rights.

Economic Inclusion & Sustainable Development

Critical analysis of Ambedkar's economic vision, including inclusive growth and economic equality.

Education as a Tool for Empowerment

Examining education as a cornerstone for empowerment and social transformation of marginalized communities.

Constitutional Values & Global Governance

Investigating Ambedkar's constitutional principles (democracy, rule of law) in global frameworks.

Intersectionality in Social Movements

Integrating caste, class, gender, and social identities in modern justice campaigns.

Important Dates

Abstract Deadline 30th June 2026
Acceptance Notification 15th July 2026
Full Paper Deadline 15th August 2026
Conference Date 18th Sept 2026

Submission Guidelines

  • Abstracts: 300-500 words, outlining central arguments, methodology, and relevance.
  • Full Papers: 4,000-6,000 words, academic formatting (upon acceptance of abstract).
  • Language: Must be in English and original research work.
  • Biography: Max 150 words of authors included with submission.
